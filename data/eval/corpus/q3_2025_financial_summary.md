# Q3 2025 Financial Summary

**Period:** 1 July 2025 – 30 September 2025
**Prepared:** 12 October 2025
**Audience:** Board, internal leadership

## Headline metrics

| Metric | Q3 2025 | Q2 2025 | Δ |
|---|---|---|---|
| Recognized revenue | $3.7M | $2.9M | +27.6% |
| Net cash burn (avg/mo) | $1.8M | $1.6M | +12.5% |
| Cash on hand (end) | $9.4M | $14.8M | -$5.4M |
| Headcount (end) | 87 | 71 | +16 |

## Revenue commentary

Q3 revenue came in at $3.7M against a plan of $4.1M. The miss is attributable in roughly equal parts to (a) the SLA service credits issued under IR-2025-014 ($420K — see incident report), and (b) the deferral of one DC#5 acceptance milestone into Q4 because of customer scheduling.

**Customer concentration remains a key risk.** 62% of Q3 recognized revenue came from a single customer (FreightCo). The board has previously asked that this be brought below 50% by end of FY2026.

## Spending

- R&D: 71% of opex (steady vs. Q2)
- Customer success and field deployment: 13%
- G&A: 11%
- Sales: 5%

The Q3 headcount jump (+16) is concentrated in engineering (12 of 16) ahead of the SR-3 alpha milestone. Recruiter capacity is the binding constraint on further growth in Q4.

## Capitalization recap

Series A raised **$14.5M** in Q1 2024 at a $58M post-money valuation. Ridgeline Ventures led; Foundation Park Capital and three angel investors participated. As of 30 September 2025, $9.4M of Series A capital remains.

## Forward look

At the current burn rate of ~$1.8M/month and the Q3-end cash position of $9.4M, runway extends approximately 5 months from the close of Q3 — i.e., through late February 2026 — without any further revenue. Factoring in conservative Q4 revenue ($4.0M plan) and Q1 2026 revenue, runway extends meaningfully but the company will need to either close a Series B or reach default-alive in 2026.
