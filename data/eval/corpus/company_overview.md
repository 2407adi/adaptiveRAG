# Solstice Robotics — Company Overview

**Last revised:** 2025-09-30

## Mission

Solstice Robotics builds general-purpose bipedal humanoid robots for warehouse and last-mile logistics environments. Our thesis: warehouses were not designed for AGVs or fixed automation; they were designed for humans. A humanoid form factor unlocks the existing built environment without retrofit.

## Founding

Solstice was founded in **April 2021** by Maya Chen (CEO) and Daniel Okafor (CTO). Maya previously led the perception team at Anduril; Daniel was a principal engineer at Boston Dynamics' commercial spinout. The company is headquartered in **Austin, TX**, with a satellite hardware lab in Pittsburgh.

## Headcount

As of **30 September 2025**, Solstice has 87 full-time employees, distributed roughly:

- Engineering (HW + SW): 61
- Operations and customer success: 14
- G&A and recruiting: 9
- Executive: 3

We operate a **remote-flexible** policy: engineering ICs may work remotely up to four days per week, but hardware roles and on-site customer deployment leads are expected at the Austin or Pittsburgh facility full-time.

## Capitalization

Solstice closed its **Series A in March 2024**, raising **$14.2M** at a $58M post-money valuation. The round was led by Ridgeline Ventures, with participation from Foundation Park Capital and three angel investors. The company has been operating on Series A capital since.

## Product line

- **SR-1** — internal research platform; not commercially sold.
- **SR-2** — first commercial product; in active deployment with FreightCo at three pilot sites.
- **SR-3** — next-generation platform; in development, slated for alpha customer deployment in 2026.

## Strategic partner

Our flagship customer is FreightCo, a regional freight and warehousing operator headquartered in Memphis. FreightCo signed a multi-year master services agreement with Solstice in late 2024. Operational details of that agreement are governed by a separate document.

## Values (excerpt)

We optimize for hardware reliability over feature velocity. We do not deploy code to customer fleets that has not been validated against the SR-1 reference rig. We do not ship demos as products.
