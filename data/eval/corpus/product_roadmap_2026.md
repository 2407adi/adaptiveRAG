# Solstice Robotics — 2026 Product Roadmap

**Owner:** Maya Chen (CEO), Daniel Okafor (CTO)
**Last updated:** 2025-12-02
**Confidentiality:** Internal — board-eyes-only

## Q1 2026

- **SR-2 firmware v3.5** ships to the full FreightCo fleet. *This was originally targeted for Q4 2025; the slip is a direct consequence of the regression-testing expansion mandated after IR-2025-014.*
- SR-3 systems-integration milestone (M-7): full body integrated walk on the reference rig.
- Hire: 8 engineers (4 controls, 2 perception, 2 ML), 2 customer success, 1 recruiter.

## Q2 2026

- **SR-3 alpha customer deployment.** Target customer: undisclosed (in negotiation; not FreightCo).
- SR-2 v3.6: outdoor terrain stability features.
- Hire: 8 engineers, 2 sales, 1 finance.

## Q3 2026

- **Second logistics customer goes live on SR-2.** Target: LinearChain (LOI signed Nov 2025).
- SR-3 beta program (3–5 customers).
- **FAA clearance** for outdoor SR-3 operations — required before any customer-site outdoor work. Target: end of Q3.
- Hire: 6 engineers, 2 sales, 1 G&A.

## Q4 2026

- **Series B raise.** Target: $25M at a meaningful step-up in valuation. Lead-investor conversations begin in Q1.
- SR-3 GA candidate firmware.
- Hire: 2 engineers, 1 sales.

## Annual hiring summary

24 engineers, 6 GTM (sales + customer success), 2 G&A, 1 recruiter — **total +33** against a starting headcount of 87.

## Dependencies and risks

- **FAA clearance** is the single largest schedule risk for Q3.
- The Series B timeline assumes Q1 and Q2 revenue come in at plan; a 20% revenue miss in either quarter likely pushes the raise into 2027.
- LinearChain is currently a non-binding LOI; a definitive contract is required by end of Q1 to keep the Q3 go-live.

## Out of scope for 2026

- International deployments.
- Manufacturing automation (non-logistics) verticals.
- Consumer-facing applications.
