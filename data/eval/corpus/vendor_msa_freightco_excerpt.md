# Master Services Agreement — Solstice Robotics ↔ FreightCo

**Excerpt: Sections 4 (SLA), 7 (IP), 9 (Termination)**
*This is an internal redacted excerpt. The full agreement is held by Legal.*

## 4. Service Level Agreement

### 4.1 Availability commitment

Solstice commits to **99.5% fleet availability** measured per calendar quarter, where "fleet availability" is defined as the ratio of unit-hours operationally available to scheduled unit-hours across all SR-2 units deployed at the customer's covered sites.

### 4.2 Service credits

If quarterly fleet availability falls below 99.5%, FreightCo is entitled to service credits as follows:

- **5% of the quarterly service fee** for each 0.1 percentage point (or fraction thereof) by which availability falls below the 99.5% commitment.
- **Capped at 25%** of the quarterly service fee in any single quarter.

Service credits are FreightCo's sole and exclusive remedy for SLA non-compliance. Credits are issued as offsets against the immediately following quarter's invoice.

### 4.3 Excluded events

Availability calculations exclude downtime caused by (a) FreightCo's own facility infrastructure (power, network, environmental control), (b) force majeure, and (c) scheduled maintenance windows agreed in advance.

## 7. Intellectual Property

### 7.1 Customer data

FreightCo retains all right, title, and interest in **operational data** generated by SR-2 units at FreightCo facilities, defined as: telemetry, video captured at FreightCo sites, task logs, and pick/place outcomes.

### 7.2 Solstice models

Solstice retains all right, title, and interest in **machine learning models, control software, firmware, and platform architecture**, including any improvements derived from operational data, provided such improvements are deployed in a manner that does not enable a third party to identify FreightCo or any FreightCo facility.

### 7.3 Joint developments

Any feature explicitly co-developed at FreightCo's request and funded by FreightCo (a "Sponsored Feature") shall be subject to a separate Statement of Work specifying ownership and licensing terms.

## 9. Termination

### 9.1 Term

The initial term is **24 months** from the Effective Date, with **automatic 12-month renewals** unless either party gives written notice of non-renewal at least **60 days** before the end of the then-current term.

### 9.2 Termination for convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on **90 days' written notice** at any time after the initial term.

### 9.3 Termination for cause

Either party may terminate for material breach on 30 days' written notice if the breach is not cured within the notice period.
